About me

Anchoring/Emceeing is my passion in true sense and I have managed to carry out 900+ events as a master of ceremonies for the last 10 years with confidence, poise and class. I strive for acing the art of Public Speaking and known for my impromptu nature during interactive sessions with the audience. I believe in owning the stage and having utmost professionalism in carrying out my services.

I love what I do , which is prominent the moment the microphone is in my hand , I need no limelight to have my presence felt all I need is my voice which connects the audience with me and helps in deliver exactly what is needed for the event to be called a success .

All said and done.. Now let my work do the Talking....
